Solid Pods are not dependent on the traditional internet, guided by huge DNS-servers. Instead, people can use P2P or the potentially faster NKN (New Kind of Network) to connect. Also useful in case of emergencies, like local power or internet outages, especially when there is an existing local Pod infrastructure
